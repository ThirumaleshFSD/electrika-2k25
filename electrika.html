About The Fest

Electrika 2K25 - Where Innovation Meets Celebration

Our department fest is an exciting annual event that brings together students, faculty, and staff for a day of fun, learning, and camaraderie. This festival highlights the creativity, talent, and hard work of everyone in the department. It's a day filled with competitions, games, performances, and workshops that showcase a wide range of skills.

This year's theme "Electrika" represents the electrifying energy and innovative spirit of our department. We have planned an exciting lineup of technical and non-technical events that will challenge your mind and entertain your soul.
